Hanyang Zhao

Hi! I am Hanyang, a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the Department of IEOR at Columbia University. I am fortunate to be advised by Professor Wenpin Tang and Professor David D. Yao. Prior to pursuing my Ph.D., I obtained my B.S. degree in Applied Mathematics at Fudan University and my M.S. in Financial Engineering also at Columbia.

I am interested in generative models, including LLMs and diffusion models. Recently, I am working on generative models alignment (RLHF).
